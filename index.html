The velocity premium

Every object below carries the exact same kinetic energy. The only question is how it gets there: with brute mass, or with raw speed. Because KE = ½mv², holding energy constant forces m ∝ 1/v² — a hyperbola that plunges as speed rises.

A 1.8-gram paperclip at meteor speed packs the same punch as a 20-ton truck at 10 m/s.

Same energy. A mass ratio of roughly 11,000,000 : 1. That collapsing curve is why a sliver of fast matter rivals something eleven million times heavier — and why the Cape Cod rock was dangerous at all.
